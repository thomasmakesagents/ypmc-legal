YPMC Media LLC ("YPMC," "we," "us," or "our") operates the YPMC Agent platform, an AI-powered legal case management system provided on a business-to-business basis to law firms and legal service organizations (collectively, "Clients"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information in connection with our services.

Important: YPMC Agent is deployed within each Client's own technology environment. YPMC does not store, access, or process end-user personal data or protected health information (PHI) on its own infrastructure. All case data remains within the Client's controlled environment.

1. Information we collect

As a B2B technology provider, we collect the following categories of information:

  • Business contact information — names, email addresses, phone numbers, and job titles of Client employees and administrators who access our platform
  • Account and billing information — company name, billing address, and payment details for service agreements
  • Usage and telemetry data — system performance metrics, error logs, and feature usage statistics that do not contain personally identifiable information
  • Communications — emails, messages, and support requests sent to YPMC

2. SMS messaging and communications

YPMC Agent uses SMS messaging to facilitate communications between law firm staff, clients, and the AI agent. In connection with SMS services:

  • Phone numbers are collected and stored within the Client's own environment
  • SMS messages are transmitted via Twilio, Inc. in accordance with their privacy policy and applicable carrier regulations
  • All SMS recipients must have provided prior consent to receive messages
  • Recipients may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message
  • Message and data rates may apply

3. How we use information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the YPMC Agent platform
  • Process payments and manage Client accounts
  • Provide technical support and respond to inquiries

4. Data we do not collect

Because YPMC Agent is deployed within each Client's own Azure environment, YPMC does not collect, store, or have access to:

  • Protected health information (PHI) of any individuals
  • Legal case files, documents, or case-specific data
  • Personal information of law firm clients or patients
  • Communications between the agent and end users

5. Information sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with:

  • Service providers — third-party vendors who assist in operating our business, including Twilio (SMS), Microsoft Azure (cloud infrastructure), and Anthropic (AI processing), each subject to appropriate data processing agreements

7. Data retention

We retain business contact and account information for the duration of the Client relationship plus seven years for legal and accounting purposes. Usage telemetry is retained for 90 days.

SMS Consent & Opt-In Policy

Effective Date: April 10, 2026

This page documents how YPMC Media LLC and its Clients collect and manage consent for SMS communications sent through the YPMC Agent platform.

How consent is collected

All individuals who receive SMS messages through YPMC Agent have provided prior consent through one or more of the following methods:

  • Verbal consent — collected during initial client intake or staff onboarding, documented in the Client's records
  • Written consent — included in client engagement agreements or staff employment documentation
  • Digital opt-in — through a consent form or acknowledgment during account setup

Who receives messages

YPMC Agent sends SMS messages to two categories of recipients:

  • Law firm staff — attorneys and paralegals who receive case notifications, alerts, and briefings as part of their job function. Consent is obtained during employment onboarding.
  • Legal clients — individuals with active legal matters who receive case status updates. Consent is obtained during the client intake process.

Message types

Recipients may receive the following types of messages:

  • Case status updates and milestone notifications
  • Document receipt confirmations
  • Follow-up reminders and deadline alerts
  • Agent responses to staff inquiries
  • Morning briefings for assigned staff

Message frequency

Message frequency varies based on case activity. Staff members may receive multiple messages per day during active periods. Clients typically receive updates at key case milestones.

Opt-out instructions

To stop receiving messages at any time, reply STOP to any message. You will receive one confirmation message and no further messages will be sent. To resume messages, reply START.

Help

Reply HELP to any message for assistance, or contact your firm administrator. You may also email us at support@ypmc.one.

Costs

Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier plan.

Privacy

We do not share your phone number with third parties for marketing purposes. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
